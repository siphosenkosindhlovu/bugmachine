iAccount - Everday Banking for Everyday People

The Steward Bank iAccount is a revitalised personal current account bundled with other Econet group products to offer a unique value proposition to you, the customer, while retaining the basic features of a current Account

Benefits and Features

  • No minimum balance
  • No credit interest
  • Debit interest - PLR plus 10% margin
  • Withdrawal Fee - 1% of minimum amount $5
  • Monthly maintenance fee - $5
  • Steward Bank proprietory card
  • Access to Internet Banking

Customers also have access to the following:

  • FREE Funeral Cover of $500 (as llong as the account stays active)
  • Automatic registration on EcoCash Banking Services
  • Access to EcoCash loans and other Econet group services.
  • Access to the MasterCard debit card
  • Option to add-on other Econet group products such as Steward Health, EcoSure Auto, Connected Car, Credit Cards, etc.
